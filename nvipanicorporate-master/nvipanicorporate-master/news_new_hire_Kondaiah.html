nVipani welcomes Kondaiah Bommisetty as CTO

Bengaluru, November 15, 2018: nVipani is proud to announce and welcome Kondaiah Bommisetty Chief Technology Officer. Kondaiah will lead all aspects of the company’s technological development, help establish the company’s technical vision and technology roadmap, and make determinations on the probability of implementation by analyzing leading-edge technologies.

Kondaiah joins the nVipani team after 13 years at Manhattan Associates. Manhattan Associates is a leading provider of supply chain management solutions. He has more than 12 years of experience in omnichannel order management software engineering and implementations. He has 18 Years of software engineering and management experience. Kondaiah holds a Bachelor of Technology degree from Sri Venkateswara University College of Engineering, Tirupati, India.

This appointment follows the opening nVipani’s new office, incorporating nVipani’s first overseas location in the US, and the launch of valUchain™, nVipani’s cloud-based, Blockchain and IoT-enabled smart supply chain management (SCM) solution.

Kondaiah says "I am thrilled to be part of the nVipani team. My entire career has been spent in the SCM industry and I look forward to continuing that tradition by providing "best of the breed" SCM solutions to nVipani’s current and future customers."

Dilli Babu, the CEO and founder of nVipani is excited about the new addition to his team. He said “Everyone at nVipani is looking forward to working with an industry veteran like Kondaiah. Not only will he bring a tighter focus our technical roadmap, but also be a great role model for our employees who come into contact with him. His deep and diverse background in Supply Chain Management and technology.”

nVipani Technology Solutions Private Limited was founded in 2015 by Dilli Babu Nandarapu and Hema Reddy. nVipani is a startup that wants to make supply chain management ubiquitous, affordable, and easy to use. nVipani wants to remove blind spots in the supply chain, connect even the most remote, rural production units to the global market, and achieve maximum efficiency at all levels in the supply chain. nVipani’s product, valUchain™ takes supply chain traceability to the next level. To know more about the company and its products, visit www.nvipani.com and www.valuchain.in.

Contact nVipani at info@nvipani.com
